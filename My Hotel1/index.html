Our Legacy

Nestled in the heart of a breathtaking landscape, Grand Luxe offers an unparalleled experience of comfort and elegance. With a commitment to exceptional service since 1925, we ensure that every moment of your stay is memorable.

Our establishment combines traditional charm with modern amenities to create a unique atmosphere that captivates our guests from around the world.
